Your Type

Quickly determine your socionic type, or the type of another person, by answering a few questions.

Instructions

The test contains one question for each dichotomy or group, but:

  • You don't need to answer all the questions, choose only those that suit you
  • Feel free to skip questions you are unsure about
  • You can change your answer to any question
  • With each choice you make, the number of possible types will decrease
  • When there is only one type left, the answers to all questions will be assigned automatically
